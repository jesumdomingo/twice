About "Twice"

『いい音楽で一度、素晴らしいパフォーマンスで二度魅了させる』

Twice is a nine-member girl group from Asia, who made their Japanese debut on June 28th, 2017, with their album『#TWICE』.


They have caused something of a social phenomenon, boasting overwhelming popularity with a range of age groups and a core focused around those in their teens. They were also invited to perform on the Japanese end-of-year TV program, Kohaku Uta Gassen for three years running from their debut and have broken a number of records with musical releases.
